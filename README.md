## What I do well in this area
- Run AI-assisted workflows on messy real-world inputs without losing quality or introducing drift
- Build prompt templates and SOPs that keep output consistent across sessions and reduce rework
- Maintain clean, usable data through standardization, deduping, required fields, and naming conventions
- Design operational systems and finish them without scope creep
- Troubleshoot issues and document root causes so the same problem doesn't surface twice

The Relocation Roadmaps samples come from a real project (https://relocationroadmaps.com) and are shared to show process, structure, and decision-making, not as polished client deliverables. The Chargeback Defender samples are from a live product built and shipped in 9 days (https://guideworks.co/chargeback-defender).
